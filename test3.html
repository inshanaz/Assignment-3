Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

by Jean Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical atist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres and today hangs in the lovere. It is the hird of three portraits of the Riviere family the artist painted that year.



Date: 1806
Medium: Old canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70cm
Home: Marie Du Lovere, Paris
